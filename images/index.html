!Egg Spaceship Nightlight!

About the Egg Spaceship Nightlight:

OUTLINE: People who are afraid of the dark might need the nightlight to be able to sleep in comfort of a warm white LED light without the fear of the darkness consuming them in the night time while they are asleep. And for the design, this is based off the Egg Spaceship in the show of “Inanimate Insanity”, so for people who are fans of the animated show, or might like spaceships in general, might be interested in using this nightlight!

REQUIREMENTS: Must light up on command using a switch, no flickering lights, must be able to stand up (we will make a nest for it to be able to stand up), everything must work smoothly, measurements are: 10 cm in height, 11 cm in width, and 10 cm in length, and for the circuit, the colour of the LED light must be white, or a warm colour of some kind.

SPECIFICATIONS: The nightlight will be in the shape of an egg, with a black casing that will have white translucent paper that will support the case. It will also be able to stand up properly with a nest that we will make out of sticks and cotton, and to add in with the futuristic look we will add some electronic components into the nest that will surround the Egg Spaceship Nightlight like how there would be worms surrounding the egg before the egg hatches into a baby bird, so the baby bird feeds on the worms. With that as well, we will need a battery pack that can be able to change the batteries, since the nightlight will work on batteries, and a functioning circuit, blending in both Digital Technologies and Electronics smoothly.

The Egg Spaceship Nightlight looks like the image to the left, but instead of a neon green light it would be a nice warm white colour. Simple!

About the details of the Egg Spaceship Nightlight:

THINGS WE MIGHT NEED TO GET: Well, considering the aesthetic of a 'techy-egg resting on the rest, futuristic' look, then for the nest, we'd use literal sticks! And place the Egg Spaceship Nightlight in the nest there, along with a few unused electronic components so they can be reused instead of thrown away in the rubbish, making it ever so slightly sustainable since we're re-using some equipment.

WHAT WE NEED TO CONSTRUCT THE NIGHTLIGHT (COMPONENTS): As for the components that we would need to construct the circuit that will function as the LED that gives the nightlight the light in the first place, we would need: a photoresistor, LED lights (the colour will be white), power source (battery pack), transistor (to control the LED), jumper wires, mini-breadboard, Arduino, resistor, double-A batteries and 10 V.

EARLY CONCEPT/DESIGN: For the early concept, we initially thought of making a simple egg nightlight, no techy-related theme to it, the early concept would initially have been a white egg with rather small and inaccurate measurements of 5 cm x 5 cm x 5 cm, which would result in a rather spherical than ovoid shape. But over time, with taking some inspirations from animated shows and building up on the theme, we decided to include a nest (which would be made out of literal sticks), and since the name of the project was "Egg Spaceship Nightlight", we took the inspirations from the animated show "Inanimate Insanity" and based the idea of the Egg Spaceship Nightlight based on the spaceship that would've been introduced in Episode 12; 'Hatching the Plan', and since spaceships are often associated with advanced technology-related ideas, we decided that along with the nest that would help support the Egg Spaceship Nightlight up, we would add some other electronic components in it (LEDs, wires, transistors, etc) to give it a more advanced and electronic-related aesthetic to it.
